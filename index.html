The Cross-Chain Operating System

1OS is a fully comprehensive operating system, which allows users to access their favorite Web3 protocols and D'Apps, across several blockchain networks - all from one interface. This helps resolve the issues caused by a currently very fragmented Web3 infrastructure, giving users a highly focused, engaging and interactive experience.

Connect to Web3 ecosystems

Powered by Polkadot

1OS securely connects users around the world to Decentralized Apps and Protocols, from DeFi and NFTs to Blockchain Gaming, across supported Blockchain Networks, through a single, decentralized operating system - utilizing Polkadot's Substrate, the interoperability framework.

1OS Team

Kristopher Ian Leo
Founder

Kristopher Ian Leo, better known simply as "Leo", is the Founder of 1OS.

Leo's technical background consists of a plethora of disciplines, including programming, design and leadership. Leo's main experience has predominantly been gained as a Software Engineer for 10+ years, with a combined high-level of expertise in UI/UX Design.

Leo's career includes long-term periods with financial institutions such as Goldman Sachs, Deloitte & BNP Paribas and having delivered Software Solutions for Apple, Emirates, Bosch, Vodafone, H&M and several other commercial brands.

Leo's exposure to programming languages and technologies include:

  • Blockchain & Clientside
    Solidity, JavaScript, TypeScript, ReactJS, Angular, VueJS
  • UI/UX Design
    HTML5, CSS3, SASS / SCSS, Adobe Design Suite
  • API & Serverside
    NodeJS, Java, .NET, PHP, Perl, CMS, Ecommerce, API Design, RESTful API, SOAP API
  • Management
    Directorship, Dev Lead experience, Product Ownership and Project Management experience
